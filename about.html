About Us

GIL- Growmore International Ltd.

A Brand you can Trust

GIL® was born in 1994. Engaged in the manufacturing of leather accessories, GIL®, has started its own in-house brand to provide customers with authentic products without the retail markup.

GIL® is your go-to brand for leather belts, leather wallets and other leather accessories.

Mission & Vission

“Clothes are like a good meal, a good movie, great pieces of music.” — Michael Kors

GIL® believes in providing customers with genuine leather accessories at an affordable price.

With Indian market being crowded with various quality of leather, GIL® takes pride in high-quality leather it uses to make its products.

GIL® produces its own leather and also imports leather from Spain.

Our Objectives

Our entire business approach is centered around our valuable customers. Their satisfaction is of utmost priority to us. To maintain a happy and satisfied customer base, we practice the following steps to the core

  • Follow stringent quality control procedures on each stage to provide only flawless products to the customers.
  • Maintaining standards in our process to the utmost satisfaction of our clients.
  • Implementing the process for continual improvement.
  • Providing ecological infrastructure & adequate training to our workers.
  • Constantly monitoring our suppliers & guiding them to supply quality products.
  • Ensuring that the undertaken orders are completed and delivered on time.
  • Giving foremost importance to customer feedback and suggestions for further improvement.
  • Full care is taken that all the leather is as per the EU/CPSAI norms including REACH.
